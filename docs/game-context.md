# Game Context - Future Development

## Player Economy
- Each account has their own "credits" currency
- Players can buy ships and equipment from certain planets
- Planets have limited amount of equipment and cargo
- Equipment/cargo restocks either every turn or per several turns

## Planet Commerce
- Double-click on a planet shows list of colonies or cities
- Most colonies are AI owned, some are player owned
- Colonies generate credits or equipment depending on buildings constructed

## Buildings & Production
- Players construct buildings on colonies
- Buildings determine what resources are generated
- Different building types produce different outputs (credits, equipment, etc.)

## Fleet Management (CRUD)
- Create, Read, Update, Delete fleet operations
- Each fleet belongs to a player/account
- Fleets can travel between systems
- Ships can be purchased and equipped
